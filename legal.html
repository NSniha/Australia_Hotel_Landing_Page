Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Australia Hotel collects information that helps us process reservations, provide guest support, improve our website, protect our services, and communicate important booking updates. We aim to collect only what is reasonably needed for these purposes.

Personal Details

Name, email address, phone number, account details, guest name, and basic contact information used for booking and support.

Stay Information

Destination, selected room, check-in and check-out dates, guest count, special requests, and reservation preferences.

Payment Metadata

Payment status, billing confirmation, transaction references, and invoice records. Full card numbers are handled by secure payment providers.

Website Usage

Device type, browser, pages visited, session activity, approximate location, and performance data used to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use personal information to deliver the service you request, maintain smooth booking operations, improve website reliability, and keep guests informed before, during, and after their stay.

  • Process, manage, modify, and confirm hotel reservations.
  • Send booking confirmations, receipts, reminders, and stay-related updates.
  • Respond to guest questions, complaints, support requests, and service issues.
  • Improve website performance, search results, booking flow, and guest experience.
  • Protect against fraud, unauthorized activity, misuse, and technical abuse.
  • Maintain business records required for accounting, tax, dispute, and legal purposes.
  • Send optional promotional emails only where permitted or where the guest has opted in.

High-Security Zone

Personal data is protected through secure transmission, access control, responsible retention, internal handling rules, and trusted service providers.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

Depending on your location and the service requested, we may process your information because it is needed to complete a booking contract, comply with legal obligations, protect legitimate business interests, prevent fraud, or act on your consent.

  • Contract: To complete and manage your hotel reservation.
  • Legal obligation: To maintain required records and respond to lawful requests.
  • Legitimate interest: To improve services, prevent abuse, and protect our platform.
  • Consent: For optional newsletters, promotional messages, or non-essential cookies.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited booking information with hotels, payment processors, technology providers, support tools, analytics services, or legal and regulatory authorities when necessary.

5. Data Retention

We keep booking, support, payment, and business records only for as long as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and maintain accurate business operations.

6. Your Privacy Rights

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion where legally and operationally possible.
  • Object to certain processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent for optional marketing communications.
  • Request a copy of your data in a structured format where applicable.

7. Contact for Privacy Requests

Privacy questions, access requests, correction requests, or deletion requests can be sent through our contact page. We may need to verify your identity before processing privacy requests.

Cookie Notice

1. What Cookies Do

Cookies are small files stored on your browser or device. They help websites remember preferences, support secure features, understand usage, and improve speed and reliability.

2. Types of Cookies We May Use

Essential Cookies

Required for navigation, security, forms, account access, and booking functions.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand visitor behavior, page performance, and website improvements.

Preference Cookies

Remember selected options such as dates, destination, currency, or form settings.

Marketing Cookies

May help show relevant offers, measure campaigns, or personalize content where allowed.

3. Why We Use Cookies

  • Keep the website secure and functional.
  • Remember booking preferences during a session.
  • Measure page loading, errors, and user experience.
  • Understand which destinations or pages guests find useful.
  • Improve search, booking forms, and website design.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be provided by trusted analytics, payment, security, or advertising partners. These providers may process limited technical information according to their own privacy and cookie policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can disable or delete cookies in your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect login, booking forms, saved preferences, or security features.

6. Cookie Updates

We may update this notice if we change cookie tools, analytics providers, marketing technology, or website functionality.

Refund Policy

1. Cancellation Rules

Refund eligibility depends on the room rate, property rules, cancellation deadline, payment terms, and booking conditions shown before confirmation. Guests should review all booking details carefully before completing a reservation.

2. Refundable Reservations

If your booking allows free cancellation, you may cancel within the stated cancellation window and receive a refund according to the displayed policy. Refund processing may depend on bank and payment provider timelines.

3. Non-Refundable Reservations

Some discounted, promotional, last-minute, or special-rate reservations may be non-refundable. These conditions are displayed before booking confirmation and may apply even if plans change.

4. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may apply when only part of a stay is cancelled, when a hotel approves an exception, or when local rules require a specific refund outcome. Any applicable fees may be deducted from the refund amount.

5. No-Show Policy

If a guest does not arrive and does not cancel within the permitted cancellation window, the booking may be treated as a no-show. No-show bookings may be charged in full depending on the property policy.

6. Refund Timing

  • Approved refunds are usually sent to the original payment method.
  • Processing times may vary by card issuer, bank, or payment provider.
  • Service fees, bank fees, or processing fees may not be refundable where permitted.
  • Currency exchange changes may affect the final amount received.

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact support with your booking reference, guest name, travel dates, and reason for cancellation. We may need additional details to review the request.

Security Standards

1. Secure Booking Environment

We use security-focused practices to protect booking data, support safe browsing, reduce unauthorized access risks, and maintain a reliable online reservation experience.

2. Data Protection Measures

  • Secure transmission is used where sensitive booking forms are submitted.
  • Access to guest information is limited to authorized team members and providers.
  • Payment information is processed through trusted payment service providers.
  • System access and account activity may be monitored for abuse prevention.
  • Security practices are reviewed as our website and services evolve.

3. Account Safety

Guests are responsible for keeping account credentials confidential. Use strong passwords, avoid sharing login access, and contact us if you believe your account or booking details have been accessed without permission.

4. Payment Security

We do not intentionally store full payment card numbers on our own servers. Payment details may be handled by secure payment processors that follow their own compliance and security obligations.

5. Incident Response

If we identify a security issue that affects guest information, we will take reasonable steps to investigate, contain the issue, notify affected users where required, and improve safeguards.

6. Guest Safety Tips

  • Use a strong and unique password for your account.
  • Do not share payment details through unsecured messages.
  • Check booking confirmation emails carefully.
  • Contact support if you notice suspicious activity.
